AR Model Viewer (USDZ & GLB)

Load your .glb or .usdz file to preview in 3D.
On supported devices, click the AR button to view in Augmented Reality.
Note: AR support depends on your device/browser.
- GLB files work on Android (Scene Viewer) and browsers with WebXR.
- USDZ files work for AR Quick Look on iOS Safari.
- Desktop browsers show the 3D viewer only.
